FAQ

Fair questions, straight answers

Something else on your mind? Ask it in the form below and you'll get a real reply, not an autoresponder.

Why a solo specialist instead of an agency?

Agencies quote you their senior people and staff your account with their junior ones. Here, the person who scopes the work is the person who does it. You also pay for outcomes, not account management meetings.

What does it cost?

The audit is completely free, and it tells you exactly what's worth spending on, including "nothing yet." Any work after that is quoted fixed-price up front, sized to the work: flow rebuilds are a project fee, ongoing campaigns are a monthly fee.

Is it safe to give you access to my account?

You add me as a user inside Klaviyo, the same way you'd add any team member. I'll ask for Manager access because my audit system runs on Klaviyo's official AI integration, which reads your account data through Klaviyo's own API. Nothing gets sent and nothing gets changed during an audit, and you can revoke my access in one click at any time.

You have a day job. Can you actually deliver?

Yes, because I deliberately take very few clients at a time and scope everything fixed before we start. You get dates in the proposal, and I hit them. The upside of this model for you: you're getting someone whose skills are sharpened daily on a real retention program, not someone who left the field to sell services.

Do you only work with Shopify?

Shopify is home turf, but I've worked across other ecommerce stacks. If Klaviyo connects to it, I can work with it.

What are the AI automations, really?

Small custom tools built with Claude that remove repetitive work: mining your reviews for copy that sounds like your customers, turning one piece of content into ten, and reports that write their own summary. I build these for my own workflow first, so you get the ones that survived real use.
